ABOUT US

CANINE TEAM is an organization non-profit which operates in the field of pet rescue, who has more than 150 members from all areas: retailers, publishers, advertising agencies, press broadcasters, television and radio stations.

CANINE TEAM departments:
‧ Press Department
‧ Televison Department
‧ Internet Department
‧ Radio Department
‧ Outdoor Department
‧ Social Media Department

CANINE TEAM's objective is to support the global activity of rescuing pets by establishing common methods, standards and specific performance indicators.

EVENTS


CANINE TEAM CONFERENCE - Bucharest

On Thursday, January 12, 2023, the first edition of the "CANINE TEAM" conference, an event dedicated to the Rescue Patrols from around the world, organized by CANINE TEAM, took place in Bucharest, Romania.

RESCUE PATROLS DAY - first edition - Paris

On Wednesday, July 17, 2023, the first edition of the "Rescue Patrols Day" conference took place in Paris, France, an event dedicated to the Rescue Patrols from around the world, organized by CANINE TEAM.

CANINE TEAM CONFERENCE - Thessaloniki

On Tuesday, June 20, 2023, the second edition of the "CANINE TEAM" conference, an event dedicated to the Rescue Patrols from around the world, organized by CANINE TEAM, took place in Thessaloniki, Greece.

SERVICES

CANINE TEAM services:
PRESS AUDIT ‧ represents the activity of auditing the information about the pet rescue activity in the printed publications.
PRODUCTS AUDIT ‧ the study that measures the consumption of products and services used in the field of pet rescue.
INTERNET AUDIT ‧ the activity of auditing information about pet rescue activities in online media publications.
OUTDOOR AUDIT ‧ comprehensive information about the sociodemographic profiles of people viewing billboards, which depict pet rescue activities.
INVESTMENT AUDIT ‧ a study that monitors investments in advertising animal rescue activities, publicized in the press, television, radio, internet and outdoor.

SMARTY - Labrador Retriever

Energetic, playful, with high intelligence and a well-developed sense of smell can help to discover and recover animals in difficulty.

BRAVY - Husky

Friendly, strong, independent and alert dog that is not aggressive in crowds of people or animals, can help to discover animals in difficulty and to recover them from dangerous places.

GENTLY - Jack Russel Terrier

A small, agile dog that can sneak through narrow spaces and notifies the other members of the team if it has discovered an injured animal.

STRONGY - German Sheperd

He is a persistent, brave and hardworking dog attached to all team members. He is strong and helps the other members to solve a task.

SMELLY - Rough Collie

She is calm and intelligent and becomes very vocal when she discovers an animal or a person in danger, in order to draw attention to the place where the animal is.

HELPY - Brittany

He is more sensitive, very shy, friendly, generally a quick learner and loyal and attached to the team. He can help injured animals by stopping bleeding.
