ABOUT US

CANINE TEAM is an organization non-profit which operates in the field of pet rescue, who has more than 150 members from all areas of the world: retailers, publishers, advertising agencies, press broadcasters, television and radio stations, outdoor companies and other interested third parties.

CANINE TEAM departments:
‧ Press Department
‧ Televison Department
‧ Internet Department
‧ Radio Department
‧ Outdoor Department
‧ Social Media Department

CANINE TEAM's objective is to support the global activity of patrolling and rescuing pets by establishing common methods and standards on how to measure the performance indicators.

SERVICES

CANINE TEAM services:
PRESS AUDIT ‧ represents the activity of auditing the information about the pet rescue activity in the printed publications, based on the standards and methodologies established by the members of the association.
PRODUCTS AUDIT ‧ represents the study that measures the consumption of products and services used in the field of pet rescue.
INTERNET AUDIT ‧ represents the activity of auditing information about pet rescue activities in online media publications as well as in websites from various fields (retail, production, etc.)s.
OUTDOOR AUDIT ‧ provides the media and advertising market with comprehensive information on billboard audiences and socio-demographic profiles.
INVESTMENT AUDIT ‧ represents the study that monitors investments in the advertising of animal rescue activities by companies in the press, television, radio, internet and outdoor.
